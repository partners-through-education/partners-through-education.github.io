Our Mission

Partners Through Education believes that by supporting equal access to education we can change the way the world thinks. PTE focuses its efforts on underserved areas, the communities we assist are often made up of primarily “minority” races. By giving access to underprivileged minorities I give them access to gain skills in order for them to go back to there community in order to continuously help other people. Education is an obvious tool but giving them access to achieve their own academic success bolsters them as a public figure which shines a positive perspective on their community, race, and religion as a whole.
