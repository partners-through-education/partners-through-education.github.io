Changing Education

Partners Through Education is a Registered Non-Profit Organization based in Los Angeles, California, dedicated to the success and well-being of students in need. The envision of all of our members to dedicate their time towards helping other aspiring students in need, and we need more of it. After our founding in 2016, we have steadily grown to become one of the largest student-started, student-run non-profits in California and the United States as a whole. We are continuously growing regularly gaining high schools and colleges which we refer to as the branches of our foundation. We are in the process of currently expanding our reach even farther to places outside of California and into other states, finding more and more powerful voices around the nation to help us achieve our goals in helping other students.

Mission

At Partners Through Education our goal is to take a holistic approach to learning. We believe that academic success is not only dependent on one’s intelligence but a wide combination of factors. In taking a holistic approach to education, we help underprivileged and underserved students retrace and undo the negative effects of their environment, and put them on a path for lifelong learning that will lead them to years of success.

Beliefs

Partners Through Education believes that by supporting equal access to education we can change the way the world thinks. PTE focuses its efforts on underserved areas, the communities we assist are often made up of primarily minorities and groups traditionally underrepresented in higher education. By giving access to underprivileged minorities we offer individuals the ability to gain the skills they need to reach their full potential. Education is an obvious tool for change but our holistic approach, in which we simplify, communicate, and educate, has resulted in real, community-wide change.

Purpose

Partners Through Education’s purpose is to affordably overcome the societal barriers that often impede and prevent students from reaching their dreams of higher education. We make it our mission to make higher education attainable to everyone, no matter one’s race, religion, socioeconomic status, or sexual orientation. To accomplish this goal PTE develops and distributes educational resources covering the finances, test preparation, college admissions, and mentorship.
