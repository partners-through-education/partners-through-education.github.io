Donate

By giving your tax-deductible donation, you help disadvantaged students who are unable to pay for the expenses of a secondary education. There is no minimum donation amount, however we urge you to donate the most that you can afford. All donations are appreciated, and you will be placed on our Contributor’s List, unless you wish to remain anonymous.

Thank you for making the right choice!
